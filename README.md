# AI Readiness Audit

A simple tool to check how well a website is understood by AI systems like ChatGPT

## Live Demo
https://ai-readiness-audit-two.vercel.app/

## What it does
Users enter a website URL and get:
- An AI readiness score (out of 100)
- imp issues that may affect how AI interprets the site

## How it works
This is a small mock audit (not real AI analysis)

The score is generated based on common content factors that influence AI understanding:-
- Clarity of product content on site
- Use of structured content (heading, section)  
- FAQ style information  
- Depth in a context

## Sample Issues Highlighted
- Missing FAQ
- Weak heading  
- Lack of structured content  
- Product messaging is not clear

## Why this matters
AI tools are becoming a discovery point. If a product is not easily understood by AI, then it reduces the chances of being recommended by AI
If it recognizes things well , then it will really boost our site or aany product.
